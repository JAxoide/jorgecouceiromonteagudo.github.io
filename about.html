About

My name is Jorge Couceiro Monteagudo, a philosopher specialized in Ethics and Artificial Intelligence in military applications. I am a candidate for Doctor of Philosophy. At the moment, I am focused on researching and applying various solutions for Ethics in Artificial Intelligence, especially in military applications.

My areas of interest include exploring applications and ethical use cases, defining clear goals and purposes, addressing human-machine decision making, modeling ethics in algorithms, and understanding the general and social impact of artificial intelligence.

Throughout my academic journey, I have developed strong analytical skills and a keen interest in the ethical implications of technology. I believe that language is a powerful tool that shapes our perceptions and interactions, and I strive to explore its potential in the digital age.

I am always eager to engage with like-minded professionals and share insights on the latest developments in my field. Collaboration and continuous learning are essential to me, and I welcome opportunities that challenge me and expand my horizons.

Feel free to connect with me on LinkedIn or reach out if you’d like to discuss potential collaborations, share insights, or simply chat about Ethics and Artificial Intelligence.
